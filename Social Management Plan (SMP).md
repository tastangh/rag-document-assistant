Section 3's Response 

## Introduction 

Due to the nature of the site being island wide. there will be no overall site plan. Instead KLED will use the "pole" as a site for future reference.Jeffrey Ogden will be responsible for recording incidents involving the public or workforce. Incidents will be reported weekly in line with the KPls.During the employee induction, all new employees will be trained to LúcELEC health and safety standards. This will be recored with Form 1.Public complaints will be recorded on Form 3 and escalated or dealt with according to the managers priority and severeness of the complaint.

## Public and Worker incidents 

All incidents shall be reported to Jeffrey Ogden via the new Website on www.KLED.Ic. Form 3 shall be filled out by the public. Notices of weekly incidence shall be displayed on Islandwide's premises to help ensure awareness. Possible risks include antisocial noise, waste from work crews, poor working conditions. These will be details in the Ongoing risks assessment. Workers complaints will be dealt with using form 3
